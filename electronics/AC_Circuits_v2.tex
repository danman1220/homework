\begin{problem}[1.a]
Consider an RLC circuit with source voltage $V_s = V_0 sin(\omega t)$.
Determine the current $I = I_0 sin(\omega t + \phi)$.
\end{problem}

\begin{solution}[1.a]
Our defining differential equation is
\[
	V_S = \frac{1}{C}Q(t) + R\frac{dQ}{dt} + L\frac{d^2Q}{dt^2}
\]
\end{solution}
